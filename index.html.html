CH.2 IS MATTER AROUND US

MATTER IS A FORM OF SUBSTANCES WHICH IS MADE OF SOLID,LIQUID AND GAS
MATTER IS DIVIDED INTO TWO PARTS:
1.PURE SUBSTANCES
2.MIXTURE

MIXTURE
 MIXTURS ARE CONSTITUED BY MORE THAN ONE KIND OF PURE FORM OF MATTER
MIXTURE IS DIVIDED INTO:
1.HOMOGENOUS MIXTURE
2.HETROGENOUS MIXTURE

1. HOMOGENOUS MIXTURE

A MIXTURE WHICH HAVE A UNIFORM SOLOTION. SUCH MIXTURE IS CALLED HOMOGENOUS MIXTURE
EX: LEMONADE

2. HETROGENOUS MIXTURE

A SOLUTION WHICH HAVE DISTINCT PARTS AND HAVE A NON-UNIFORM SOLUTION. SUCH A MIXTURE IS CALLED HETROGENOUS MIXTURE

WHAT IS SOLUTION??

A SOLUTION IS HOMOGENOUS MIXTURE OF TWO OR MORE SUBSTANCES.
EX:LEMONADE

WHAT IS SUSPENSION SOLUTION

A SUSPENSION IS A HETROGENOUS SOLUTION IN WHICH SOLUTE PARTICLES DO NOT DISSOLVE. BUT REMAIN SUSPENDED THROUGOUT THE BULK OF MEDIUM.
EX:CHALK POWDER IN WATER

WHAT IS COLLOID??

A COLLOID SOLUTION IS HETROGENOUS MIXTURE.IN WHICH THE PARTICLES ARE UNIORMLY SPRAD THIS TYPE OF SOLUTION IS CALLED COLLOID SOLUTION.
EX:MILK IN WATER

TNYDALL EFFECT

TNYDALL EFFECT IS EFFECT IN WHICH A SOLUTION PARTICLE SCATTER A BEAM F LIGHT.
EX:HETROGENOUS MIXTURE
